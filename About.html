• About Me

    Welcome to Recipes by Mitesh, where culinary dreams come to life!

    I love to cook, but I haven’t always. In fact, for most of my life, cooking dinner usually meant microwaving a frozen dinner or adding boiling water to something that came from a box. It wasn’t until I setteled down, that I started cooking real food . I started realizing that cooking could be fun. Eating real food was really fun. And gaining confidence as a home chef? That was life changing!

    Recipes by Mitesh - It’s a food website, so naturally you’re going to find recipes here. But I only share the recipes I really, truly love. Step-by-step instructions. When I first started cooking, I was unsure about so many things like: How big should I dice the onions? ; How thick should I cut the vegetables? ; What should this look like halfway through the recipe when it looks NOTHING like the final photo? . I love learning any skill that helps me get the job done faster, easier or with a better result. Sometimes cooking just doesn’t go as planned but don’t let that discourage you, though. Learn from the mistakes and laugh along with me. It’s just food…if it doesn’t turn out good, toss the rest and just try again next time. Fun! Food and fun come together as everything about food should be fun: eating it, making it and even making a mess of it.

    Now, let’s go have some fun in the kitchen!
