• Cauliflower Sabzi Recipe

    Ingredients

    • 300 grms maida flour
    • 6 table spoon curd
    • 2 table spoon butter
    • 1/2 tea spoon salt
    • 1/2 tea spoon soda-bi-carb
    • 1 tea spoon baking power

    Method to Prepare Naan

    1. Mix flour, salt and baking powder
    2. Rub in the butter in the flour mix
    3. Now pour curd and soda-bi-card
    4. Mix all ingredients well to make a soft dough. Keep on adding milk slowly to make soft dough
    5. Keep aside for 4-5 hrs in warm place
    6. Now Roll out dough balls and brush with little butter
    7. Now cook on frying pan
    8. Finally Serve hot
