• Panner Makhani Recipe

    Ingredients

    • 1/2 cup Panner cubes
    • 2 tablespoons Onion paste
    • 1 teaspoon Ginger-Garlic paste
    • Pinch Turmeric powder (haldi)
    • 1/2 teaspoon Chilli powder
    • 1/4 cup Tomato puree
    • 1 teaspoon dried Fenugreek leaves (Kastoori Methi)
    • 3/4 cup Milk
    • 1 teaspoon Sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon Butter
    • 1/2 teaspoon Oil
    • Salt to taste

    Method to Prepare Panner Makhani

    1. Heat the butter and oil in a pan.
    2. Add the onion paste, ginger-garlic paste and stir for some time.
    3. Add the turmeric powder, chilli powder and tomato puree and cook till the oil separates.
    4. Finally, add the kastoori Methi, Milk, Sugar, Salt and Panner cubes and simmer for 2 to 3 minutes.
    5. Serve hot.
